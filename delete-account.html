Delete Your Mugi Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to permanently delete your Mugi account and what data will be removed.

What Will Be Deleted

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile and account info
  • Your collection, binders, and decks
  • Your trade history and wanted list
  • Your messages and conversations
  • Your tournament match history
  • Your social feed posts and stories

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Request Deletion

To request account deletion, email us at support@mugiapp.app with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We'll process your request and confirm once your account and associated data have been permanently removed.
