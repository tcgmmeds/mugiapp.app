COMPETE

Level up your game

Tools built around how competitive players actually prepare, play, and improve.

Tournament tracker

Log matches with leader selection, W/L, dice roll, first or second. Add full-page strategy notes per event. Export shareable tournament recaps as images.

Deck builder

Build decks with auto-validation — color matching, 4-copy limits, 50-card requirement. See stat breakdowns by cost, counter, and trait. Cycle through leader art variants.

Matchup notes NEW

Save strategy notes for every leader-vs-leader matchup. Track your win rate against specific leaders. Know your game plan before you sit down.

CONNECT

Find your crew

Trade, chat, and share with other players. Not another marketplace — a community.

Crew & social NEW

Add friends, post to the Grand Line feed, upvote and downvote, and DM your crew with text, cards, and images. Your community lives here.

Trade center

Propose trades with friends, track pending offers, and message to negotiate. Post wanted cards so your crew knows what you're looking for.

COLLECT

Track everything you own

Your collection, organized and priced. Binders, grading, portfolio charts — it's all here.

Binders & collection

Organize cards into custom binders with per-binder portfolio charts. Track graded cards with multi-entry grading and see their value over time.

Live market prices

TCGPlayer prices synced 4x daily across 3,500+ cards. Your portfolio updates automatically. Browse by set or check what's trending on the market tab.
