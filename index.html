Features

Everything you need in one app

📊

Collection Tracker

Add cards to your binder and watch your portfolio value update in real time with live market pricing and interactive charts.

💰

Live Market Prices

Real-time pricing for every card across all sets. See the most valuable cards, compare set totals, and never overpay again.

🃏

Deck Builder

Build and manage deck lists with a visual editor. Import existing lists or create from scratch with auto-calculated market values.

🔄

Trade Center

Post wanted cards, browse bounties from other collectors, and propose trades directly in the app with notes and reviews.

🏆

Tournament Tracker

Log results match by match. Track wins, losses, and win rates across events with leader and location details.

👥

Crew & Social

Build your crew, connect with friends, and follow their trades and activity on the Grand Line live social feed.
